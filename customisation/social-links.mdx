---
title: Setting up social links
description: 'Setting the social links for your website'
---

<Info>
    Currently we only provide the ability to provide social links for:
    - Facebook
    - GitHub
    - Instagram
    - LinkedIn (company and personal)
    - Pinterest
    - Reddit
    - Twitter
    - YouTube
    If you could like more then please suggest it through our [feedback page](https://insigh.to/b/site-seedling)
</Info>

<Steps>
    <Step title="Step 1">
        Go to your Site Seedling dashboard
    </Step>
    <Step title="Step 2">
        Open "Social Media"
    </Step>
    <Step title="Step 3">
        Click on "New Social Media"
        
        ![New Social Media Button](/images/new-social-media-button.png)
    </Step>
    <Step title="Step 4">
        Select the social media platform you would like to create a link for.

        ![Social Media Network Dropdown](/images/networks-dropdown.png)
    </Step>
    <Step title="Step 5">
        Enter your social media Username or tag.

        You only need to enter the username part of the link to your profile.
        For example, if you wanted to do a GitHub link and your profile was
        [https://github.com/Satal](https://github.com/Satal) (I doubt it as 
        that's mine) then you would enter "Satal"
    </Step>
    <Step title="Step 6">
        Click on "Create" or "Create & create another" if you would like to
        create more social links.
    </Step>
</Steps>
